About

I’m a PhD student at Queensland University of Technology, Australia, working on computer vision and machine learning. My research is on representation and reconstruction of 3D shapes. I spent a research internship semester at Facebook Reality Labs (aka Oculus Research) in 2018 and in the CI2CV lab, Robotics Institute at CMU in 2017 working on 3D reconstruction problems.
